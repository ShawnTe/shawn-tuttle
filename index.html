I’m Shawn. I have a background in professional organizing, mindful productivity, and a decade in software engineering. I’ve spent years obsessed with one question: why do things make perfect sense on paper — and fall apart in real life?

Started from zero with an instrument to performing on stage with a band. Led bicycle tours through Italy and France for two seasons. Backpacked in my 40s through Mexico, Thailand, and Europe. Left a decade of solopreneurship to become a software engineer for 10 years.

The middle part is always sticky. My overactive head goes into overdrive — who am I to do that? Too old, too unqualified, too fill-in-the-blank — until you’ve almost convinced yourself the thing isn’t possible.

I know what it feels like when the knowing is louder than the doubt. I’ve learned to listen for it, trust it, and move from it. And I’m ready to share that with you.

— Shawn

The Alignment Method

3 ways to win.

Single Session

The Ignition Session

This is where The Alignment Method begins: Orient. We notice where you are now — not where you think you should be. Who’s been missing from the table.

Then we invite her back in. Vision. We re-seat her at the head of the table and invite her to lead.

You’ll leave with a felt sense of her — not just clarity on direction, but a genuine reconnection with who you’re becoming. Plus a personalized toolkit: a visual, a playlist, a guided audio made for you.

For you if you know something has been pulling and you’re ready to move. You don’t want a lot of hand-holding — you just need the spark.

6 Weeks

The Tuning

Vision is back at the head of the table. Now we make sure she stays.

Over six weeks, we go deeper into The Alignment Method — clearing what’s been crowding her out, deepening the Orient work, and moving into Align: the practice of bringing your life into resonance with her.

Your actions, your mindset, your environment — reflecting Vision ever more deeply.

For you if you want the spark and the structure. You’re making a real change and you want someone in your corner while it takes hold.

12 Weeks

The Full Season

The complete Alignment Method. All twelve weeks — more angles, more depth, more room for the work to take root.

Vision is at the head of the table. Navigate is how we keep her there. Deeper self-awareness. The patterns that keep surfacing, the self-sabotage you almost didn’t catch, the integration that helps it stick.

By the end, you Are her.

For you if you’re in the middle of a real transition and you want sustained support while it unfolds. You’re not just learning a new practice — you’re becoming someone new.
