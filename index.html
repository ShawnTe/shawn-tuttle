How I guide

I’m Shawn. I’ve gone from never playing an instrument to performing on stage with a band. Led bicycle tours through Italy and France. Packed a backpack in my 40s and traveled through Mexico, Thailand, and Europe. Left a decade of solopreneurship to become a software engineer — and spent the next ten years inside the puzzle of writing code.

The pattern looks simple from the outside. The middle is always sticky. I’ve learned to hear myself through the noise, trust what I know, and move from there.

That’s what I teach.

I bring structure and reflection — so you’re not left spinning, and you’re not being told what to do. Light guidance, practical tools, and a weekly space to hear yourself more clearly.

— Shawn
